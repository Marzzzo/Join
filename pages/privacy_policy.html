Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 28, 2025

Welcome to Join (join-418.de), a Kanban board platform designed to help users manage tasks efficiently. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you register and use Join, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and password (encrypted).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the platform, such as tasks created or boards used.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Join platform.
  • Allow you to log in and manage your Kanban boards.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates or changes to the service.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users and the platform.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Passwords are encrypted, and data transmission is secured using industry-standard protocols.

5. Your Rights

As an EU-based user, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or deletions of your personal data.
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing (where applicable).

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@join-418.de.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our platform. By using Join, you consent to the use of cookies. You can manage cookie settings in your browser at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
